Services

CUSTOM DESIGN

We design for those that seek elegance and luxurious sophistication in their custom designed piece. Reflecting your personal style, story and spirit, we take you on a journey of self expression, embedding your story amongst the sparkly facets. We get so much joy from building relationships with our clients, ensuring your piece is intertwined with your identity.

JEWELLERY POLISHING AND CLEANING

Make an appointment for us to inspect your piece, where we will discuss the appropriate solution and technique to clean and polish your treasured item and bring it back to life.

Recycle your gold

Using gold you may already own to create new jewellery is an inexpensive alternative to using new Gold. Our bespoke jewellery service can create you a new piece of jewellery from your old gold, diamonds and gemstones, many of the pieces you see on our site have been created this way.
